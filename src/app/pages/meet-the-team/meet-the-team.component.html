Meet the Team

Dr. Julie LeFever DC, CACCP

Chiropractor

Dr. Julie LeFever graduated from Palmer Chiropractic College in October of 2006. She then came back to Armada, where she was born and raised, and opened Main Street Chiropractic in August of 2007. She is married to her husband, Brandon, and has two sons, Hunter and Lucas. She is very active in the community and belongs to the various Armada organizations. She is passionate about helping the community live symptom free without the need for medications or surgeries. She is also certified in Prenatal and Pediatric care by the ICPA.

Andrea Keisic LMT

Licensed Massage Therapist

Andrea has been with Main Street Chiropractic since September of 2007. She has been fortunate to grow and succeed over the years along side of Dr. Julie. She has been the only massage therapist employed at Main Street Chiropractic and has been here since the beginning. She graduated from Carnegie Institute in 2005 and has loved being able to help so many patients over the years. Andrea offers several types of massages which include relaxation, deep tissue and trigger point work. She is also prenatal certified.
